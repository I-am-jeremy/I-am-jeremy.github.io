Electron Capture Rate Calculations

In the center of pre-supernovae stars numerous reactions occur, building up to the explosion. One important reaction that can help determine behavior of the star is the rate of electron capture reactions (electron goes into tehe nucleus and combines with a proton to make a nuetron and release enrgy through neutrino emission). The nuclear shell model describes a nucleus as having different levels of energy for a nucleun to occupy with different possiblities of likliehood. By using the nuclear shell model my group calculated the temperature dependence of the rate of electron capture reactions. These results were published "Finite-temperature electron-capture rates for neutron-rich nuclei around N=50 and effects on core-collapse supernovae simulations" in the Physical Review C

The Stepping Stone Game

The Stepping Stone Game is a one person mathematical game played on an infinite square grid. The goal of the game is to get to the highest number possbile on the grid, while obeying the games rules. This is the research highlighted on the Youtube Chanel Numberphile, in the video with more than 240,000 views entitled "Stones on an Infinite Chessboard- Numberphile"

The rules are as follows:

1) To setup the game you place n 1's on the board (in any squares you choose)

2)To place the number k, the adjacnet 8 squares (orthogonal and diagnol) must add up to exactly to k.

3)Numbers must be placed consecutively (must have a 2 on the board before you place a 3).

4) You can only place each number once (excluding ones).

If we consider the highest number possible for each of the n amount of starting 1s we aquire a sequnce, with the first entry the highest number you can reach with 1 one , then the second with 2 starting ones, and so on. We calculated the first few entries to this sequence and submitted it to the online encyclopedia for integer sequences (the OEIS), where it was published and expanded on. The original sequence is A337663 , and some adaptations are A340000 , A342431 , and A342434
